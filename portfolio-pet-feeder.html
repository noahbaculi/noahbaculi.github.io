Automatic Pet Feeder

Designer and Engineer, 2023

My family had three cats, all with different feeding schedules. After seeing the price of commercial automatic feeders, I set out to design my own. I first experimented with different ideas for the sensing and to ensure the electronics would work as expected.

I ended up settling on using an ESP32 WROOM microcontroller and a bluetooth tag from Blue Charm Beacons to trigger the opening and closing of the door. I wrote the Arduino (C++) code to manage the logic and it is hosted on my Github.
